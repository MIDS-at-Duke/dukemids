Duke MIDS Student Resources

Looking for information about the Duke MIDS program?

If you are a prospective student or are looking for general information about the Duke Master in Interdisciplinary Data Science program, please visit datascience.duke.edu.

This site contains supplemental resources for current MIDS students only.

Data science is an intrinsically applied field — but graduate programs often focus on methods and theory while leaving students to discover on their own the practical skills, professional habits, and career wisdom that make a real difference. This site collects that hard-won knowledge in one place.

These resources are curated for students in the Duke Master in Interdisciplinary Data Science (MIDS) program, drawing on years of teaching, advising, and mentoring MIDS students through their degrees and into their careers.
